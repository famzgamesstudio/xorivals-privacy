This Privacy Policy describes how Famz Games Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use XO Rivals ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01

Information We Collect

Information you provide
  • Display name and avatar color chosen during profile setup
  • Profile photo if you voluntarily upload one
  • In-game chat messages sent during online matches
  • Player names submitted to the leaderboard after a game
Information collected automatically
  • Anonymous Firebase Authentication UID (generated automatically — no email or password required)
  • Game data: moves, match results, ELO ratings, tournament records
  • Device information: OS version, device model (collected by Firebase and AdMob)
  • IP address and approximate location (country level) for matchmaking and ad targeting
  • FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) token for push notifications
  • Advertising ID (GAID) used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads
  • Crash reports and performance data via Firebase Crashlytics
✅

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or payment information unless you voluntarily provide them when contacting us for support.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Purpose Data used Legal basis
Run online multiplayer matches Firebase UID, game state Contractual necessity
Display player profiles and leaderboards Display name, avatar, ELO rating Legitimate interest

03

Google AdMob & Advertising

XO Rivals uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to show you personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your consent and region.

What AdMob collects
  • Google Advertising ID (GAID) to identify your device for ad personalization
  • IP address to determine your approximate location for regional ad targeting
  • Device information (OS, model, screen size) for ad rendering
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks) for billing and reporting
  • App usage data and in-app events for ad relevance
⚠️

Google AdMob is a third-party service operated by Google LLC. We do not control what data Google collects or how it is processed. Google's use of advertising data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Ad consent & your choices
  • On first launch, you will be shown a consent dialog (Google UMP — User Messaging Platform) to choose between personalized and non-personalized ads
  • You can reset or change your ad preferences at any time in your device settings under Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID or Delete Advertising ID (Android 12+)
  • To opt out of personalized ads globally, visit adssettings.google.com
  • Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland will see a GDPR-compliant consent form before any ads are shown

Ad revenues help us keep XO Rivals free for all players. We appreciate your support.

04

Firebase & Third-Party Services

XO Rivals is built on Google Firebase. The following Firebase services are used and may process your data:

Service Purpose Data processed
Firebase Authentication Anonymous sign-in for online rooms Firebase UID
Cloud Firestore Game rooms, chat, profiles, leaderboard Game data, display name, chat messages
Firebase Realtime Database Low-latency online matches Game state, moves
Firebase Storage Avatar photo uploads Profile photos (if uploaded)
Firebase Cloud Messaging Push notifications FCM device token
Firebase Crashlytics Crash and error reporting Device info, stack traces
Google AdMob In-app advertising Advertising ID, device info

All Firebase services are operated by Google LLC and are subject to the Google Cloud Privacy Notice and the Google Privacy Policy. Data may be stored on servers located in the United States and other countries where Google operates data centers.

05

Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between the App and Firebase is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Firebase Security Rules are in place to ensure users can only read/write their own data
  • Avatar photos stored in Firebase Storage are access-controlled — only authenticated users can read them
  • Match history is stored locally on your device using Android DataStore and is not accessible to other apps
  • We do not sell your personal data to any third parties
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties except as required to operate the services described in this policy (Firebase, AdMob)
⚠️

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

06

Children's Privacy

XO Rivals is rated Everyone (E) and may be played by children. We take children's privacy seriously.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA/UK)
  • The App uses anonymous Firebase Authentication — no email address or real name is required to play
  • Online chat is available only during active matches. We do not moderate chat in real time but provide a reporting mechanism
  • Google AdMob is configured to serve non-personalized ads only when the App detects it may be used by a child, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google's Families Policy
ℹ️

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at famzgamesstudio@gmail.com and we will delete the information promptly.

07

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your region, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

Rights available to all users
  • Delete your data — You can delete your match history directly within the App (History → Clear All). To delete your online profile and Firebase data, contact us
  • Disable push notifications — Turn off notifications in your device's App Settings at any time
  • Opt out of personalized ads — Reset or delete your Advertising ID in device settings, or use the in-app consent controls
  • Delete your avatar — Remove your uploaded photo through Profile Settings in the App
Additional rights (EEA / UK / California residents)
  • Right of access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — Object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing
  • Right to withdraw consent — Withdraw consent for ad personalization at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at famzgamesstudio@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
